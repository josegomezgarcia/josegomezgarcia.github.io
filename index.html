Academic CV in French (2024/02)

I am currently an assistant professor (maître de conférences) in Statistics and Data Sciences (SD) at the MAP5 lab and the SD department of the IUT de Paris - Rives de Seine, Université Paris Cité.

My teaching responsibilities in the Data Science department focus primarily on statistical learning and time series analysis. Before joining the MAP5 lab and the IUT de Paris, I was a Lecturer and Researcher at ENSAI and the CREST lab (2023-2024) and at AgroParisTech/INRAE/Université Paris-Saclay (2020-2023), where I concentrated on teaching and researching in statistical modeling of environmental and biological data.

I also completed a postdoctoral fellowship in machine learning at LMNO/ UNICAEN, working on mixture models and deep neural network-based models. During this period, I co-organized the Statistics and Data Science Working Group and participated in various teaching activities at UNICAEN and ENSAI.

My academic background includes a Ph.D. in Statistics-Mathematics from CY Cergy Paris Université, under the supervision of Paul Doukhan, and a research master’s in stochastic geometry from the Central University of Venezuela, supervised by José R. (Chichi) León.

Current research interests

    • Applications to climatic and environmental risks
  • Stochastic processes

  • Statistical learning
